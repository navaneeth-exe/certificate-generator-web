📋 Detailed Instructions: How to prepare your certificate

Step 1: Design your Certificate

Create your certificate design in Canva, PowerPoint, or any design tool. Make sure to leave an empty space where the person's name will go.

Step 2: Add the Magic Tag

In your design, type exactly {NAME} or {{NAME}} where you want the participant's name to appear. The software will automatically replace this tag with real names later.

Step 3: Export as PowerPoint

You must export or download your final design as a PowerPoint Presentation (.pptx) file. If you are using Canva, click Share > Download > select 'PPTX' from the dropdown.

Step 4: Upload it here

Drag and drop that .pptx file into the box above to continue!

📋 Detailed Instructions: How to provide names

You can provide your list of names in a few ways:

  • Excel or CSV: Make sure the names are in the very first column. We will automatically ignore headers like "Name" or "S.No".
  • Word Document: Just type one name per line.
  • Manual Entry: Click "Type Manually" below and paste your names straight into the box.
